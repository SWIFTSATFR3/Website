SWIFT-SAT FR3

Software Defined Radio based Emulation of SAT-Terrestrial Network Coexistence in FR3 Bands is a NSF project focused on creating an over-the-air indoor FR3 testbed for experimental studies of coexistence between terrestrial and satellite systems, using software-defined radios (SDR) to emulate dense 5G cellular networks and satellite equipment. The FR3 testbed is an extension of an existing testbed, the COSMOS sandbox at Rutgers University WINLAB, and is an open resource remotely accessible to other researchers.
